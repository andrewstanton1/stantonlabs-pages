Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 14, 2025

1. Introduction

Stanton Labs (“we,” “our,” “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our applications, including SipUs, handle your information.

2. Information We Collect

SipUs stores all hydration data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal information on our servers.

3. How We Use Your Data

Because your data never leaves your device, it is only used by the app for its intended purpose: tracking your hydration and providing reminders.

4. Third-Party Services

Our apps do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services. If you access external links (such as the App Store), those sites are governed by their own privacy policies.

5. Data Security

Since data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device’s settings, such as passcodes or biometric authentication. We recommend keeping your device software up to date.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our apps are intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on our website.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at tengis@stantonlabs.com.
